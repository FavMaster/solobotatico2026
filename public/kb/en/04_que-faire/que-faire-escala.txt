SHORT:
Around L’Escala, enjoy beaches, medieval villages, cultural sites,
nature walks, water activities and local gastronomy,
all within a radius of about 40 km.

LONG:
Around L’Escala, there is a wide variety of activities to enjoy.
Within a radius of about 40 km, you can discover culture, nature,
the sea, sports, gastronomy and typical villages of the Costa Brava.

1. Cultural and historical discoveries
Ruins of Empúries (≤ 2 km)  
Major archaeological site with Greek and Roman remains,
including a museum and direct access to the sea.

Sant Martí d’Empúries (≤ 3 km)  
Charming medieval village with narrow streets,
offering beautiful views over the Mediterranean.

Dalí Theatre-Museum in Figueres (≈ 25 km)  
The famous museum dedicated to Salvador Dalí,
a must-see for art and surrealism lovers.

Montgrí Castle (≈ 15 km)  
Medieval castle perched on a hill,
offering spectacular panoramic views of the Baix Empordà.

Girona (≈ 45 km)  
Remarkable historic city with its cathedral,
Jewish quarter and colorful houses along the Onyar River.

2. Beaches and water activities
Beaches of L’Escala  
Riells, Montgó and other beaches ideal for swimming,
sunbathing and water activities.

L’Estartit and the Medes Islands (≈ 15 km)  
Renowned spot for diving and snorkeling
in a protected marine reserve.

Roses (≈ 20 km)  
Seaside resort with long beaches,
water sports and boat excursions.

3. Nature and hiking
Montgrí Natural Park, Medes Islands and Baix Ter  
Ideal for hiking, wildlife observation
and spectacular landscapes.

Aiguamolls de l’Empordà (≈ 25 km)  
Natural wetland park,
perfect for birdwatching and peaceful walks.

4. Typical villages and Costa Brava
Pals (≈ 30 km)  
Medieval hilltop village,
cobblestone streets and preserved heritage.

Peratallada (≈ 35 km)  
Iconic medieval village,
known for its stone architecture and authentic atmosphere.

Coves and coves of the Costa Brava  
Numerous coves ideal for swimming,
snorkeling and relaxation.

5. Sports and leisure activities
Hiking and mountain biking  
Marked trails along the coast
or in the countryside.

Water sports  
Paddleboarding, sea kayaking, diving and sailing
at the L’Escala Nautical Club and nearby areas.

Horse riding  
Horseback rides along the beach
with a local partner in L’Estartit.

Golf  
Several renowned golf courses nearby,
including Empordà Golf and Golf de Pals.

6. Gastronomy and wine tourism
Local specialties  
L’Escala anchovies, cold cuts,
olive oil, Empordà wines
and Mediterranean cuisine.

Wine tastings  
Visits to vineyards and tastings
in the Empordà region.

Local markets  
Village markets to discover
fresh and local products.
